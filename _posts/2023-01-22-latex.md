# Stuff



## Finnish papers

Remember to use A4 paper, Finnish babel and fix hyphenation. Note that do not use scandinavian letters.

~~~

\documentclass[a4paper,12pt]{article}

\usepackage[finnish]{babel}

\usepackage{hyphenat}
\hyphenation{myo-hem-min}

~~~
